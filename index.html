About Me

My Name is Kayla

I am 31 years old, have two beautiful children. Born and raised in Minnesota. We love going hiking, camping, swimming, boating, fishing, ice fishing and many more. While out with the family I started taking photos of the beautiful nature that surrounded us. The picutres have actually turned out great. So, I decided to share them with others that enjoy the beauty of nature as much as I do.
